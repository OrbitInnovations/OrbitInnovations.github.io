ScholarX — One App, One System

Most MAKAUT students struggle not because they lack ability — but because everything is scattered. Notes in one place, PYQs somewhere else, syllabus buried in PDFs. ScholarX fixes that.

An all-in-one platform built specifically for MAKAUT students to prepare smarter, not harder — with organized notes, a PYQ bank to understand exam patterns, exam-focused questions, mock tests, a CGPA calculator, and full offline access. No more switching between multiple sources.
